Diagonalize representation(matrix) associated to the BilinearForm at selected dofs dofs by putting 0 on the extra diagonal terms and 1 on the diagonal.

If ON_ELIMINATION_KEEP_DIAGONAL is set in on_context then the diagonal value of the matrix is kept and the right habd side rhs is modified accordingly.
